# ah-month

Run monthly review: strategic reflection, monthly cleanup, and next-month planning.

## Must Follow

- Memory read mode: summary first across all layers.
- Template: `assets/templates/Monthly-Review-Template.md`
- Every unresolved item this month must have a decision (action/card/archive).

## Workflow

1. Summarize monthly knowledge output and project momentum.
2. Clear unresolved monthly ideas.
3. Strategic reflection: continue/stop/start.
4. Generate and write monthly review note.
5. Update monthly status in `STATUS.md` and `index.json`.

## Read References On Demand

- Strategic review dimensions and output format: `references/monthly-review-details.md`

## Output

- Monthly key outcomes and trends.
- Cleanup results for unresolved items.
- Next-month strategy and focus list.
